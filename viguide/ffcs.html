What is FFCS?

FFCS (Fully Flexible Credit System) is the course registration system used in our university. It allows students to choose their own courses, faculty, and timings based on availability and their academic requirements.

Key Features of FFCS:

  • Flexibility to choose courses across departments
  • Option to select preferred faculty members
  • Ability to create personalized timetables
  • Multiple rounds for course selection
  • Real-time seat availability updates

How to Use FFCS:

  1. Login to the university portal during registration period
  2. Browse available courses in your department
  3. Select your preferred courses and sections
  4. Submit your choices before the deadline
  5. Check the results in subsequent rounds

Important Notes:

The FFCS portal is typically open for limited periods during the semester. Make sure to complete your registration within the specified dates to avoid late registration penalties.
